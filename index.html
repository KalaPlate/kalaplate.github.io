Who Am I_

I am a student driven by a deep curiosity for digital creation. Beyond my academics, I specialize in web development and professional video editing. Whether I am designing a responsive website or crafting a compelling visual story, I am constantly pushing myself to master new skills and turn ideas into reality.

My journey began with digital storytelling through professional video editing, which sparked my curiosity to build the platforms where media lives. This led me to master front-end web development, allowing me to bridge the gap between creative visual media and clean, responsive code.

Currently, I am building applications designed to simplify everyday life, with a strong focus on low-cost, highly secure device automation. As a developer, my ultimate goal is creating flawless, "set-and-forget" systems that run seamlessly and autonomously in the background.
